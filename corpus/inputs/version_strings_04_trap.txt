The phishing campaign delivered a malicious document via a dropper agent reporting version 7.68.0.144 on hosts running Windows build 10.0.19041.1. The document pulled a payload from hxxps://mail-verify-secure[.]com/update/portal.php, and sandbox detonation showed the macro connecting straight to 185.220.101.44 immediately after the file opened. The URL and the IP were both distributed to partner SOCs.
